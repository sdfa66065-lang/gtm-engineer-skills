Content Updated Within 3 Months Earns 54% More AI Citations

Pages updated within the last 3 months average 6.0 AI citations, compared to 3.9 for content older than 2 years — a 54% advantage. AI assistants cite content that is 25.7% fresher than traditional organic search results. Based on 2.3 million pages across 295,000 domains.

Source: SE Ranking, November 2025 (seranking.com). 2.3M pages, 295K domains. Supplemented by Ahrefs, 2025 (ahrefs.com) — 17M citations across 7 AI platforms.
What this means AI engines have a strong freshness bias. Regularly updating existing content is more effective than publishing new pages infrequently. Implementing a content refresh cadence — updating key pages with new data points every 1–3 months — directly increases the likelihood of being cited by ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.
