Authoritative Quotations Lift AI Visibility by 41%

Authoritative quotations are the single most effective GEO strategy, boosting AI engine visibility by 41%. Statistics (+33%) and cited sources (+28%) round out the top three. Lower-ranked sites benefit most — pages originally ranked 4th–5th saw up to +115% improvement. Based on 10,000 queries tested across 9 generative engines.

Source: Aggarwal et al., "GEO: Generative Engine Optimization," KDD 2024 (arXiv). 10,000 queries across 9 generative engines. Each strategy applied independently to baseline content; visibility measured as impression-weighted position improvement.
What this means Content with direct quotes from named experts and specific data points earns significantly more AI citations than content optimized purely for readability or keywords. The fastest path to AI visibility is adding verifiable quotations and statistics to existing pages — not rewriting them. Keyword stuffing actively harms AI visibility, unlike traditional SEO where its effect is merely neutral.
