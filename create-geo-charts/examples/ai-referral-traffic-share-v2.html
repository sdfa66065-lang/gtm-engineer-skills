ChatGPT Drives 87% of All AI Referral Traffic to Websites

ChatGPT accounts for 87.4% of all AI-driven referral traffic, dwarfing Perplexity (6.2%), Gemini (3.1%), Claude (2.1%), and all others combined (1.2%). Based on Conductor's analysis of 13,700 domains and 3.3 billion sessions through November 2025.

Source: Conductor, "AEO/GEO Benchmarks Report," November 2025 (conductor.com). 13,700 domains, 3.3B sessions. AI referral traffic categorized by source domain.
What this means ChatGPT is not just the most popular AI assistant — it is the dominant source of AI-driven website traffic by a 14:1 margin. Optimizing content for ChatGPT citation patterns (answer-first structure, quotable blocks, comparison tables) delivers the highest ROI. Perplexity's 6.2% share is notable given its smaller user base, suggesting its users click through to sources at a higher rate.
